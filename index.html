How It Works

Three layers of
intelligent oversight.

Overview puts an AI operator on every site, a portfolio agent for every client, and a fleet command layer for your operations team. Every layer talks to the ones above and below it — knowledge flows in both directions.

Per Site — Always On
VERA
Virtual Engineering & Response Assistant

VERA lives on each building and acts as a virtual experienced operator. She onboards to a new site in under an hour, learns every piece of equipment and its normal operating range, and scans every point every 15 minutes — forever.

  • Onboards to any building — understands points automatically
  • Builds a rulebook of what normal looks like for that site
  • Maintains a site memory that grows smarter over time
  • Catches faults before any human notices
  • Sends plain-language alerts to the right people
  • Calculates and reports a live 0–100 health score
  • Emails from vera@overviewops.com — feels like a real team member
  • Chat interface — ask VERA anything about the building
Per Client Portfolio
Portfolio Agent
One agent per multi-building client

Clients with multiple buildings get their own dedicated portfolio agent. Their facilities director or ownership group asks plain-language questions and gets answers across their entire portfolio — without needing to understand BAS, BACnet, or any technical system.

  • Knows every building in the client's portfolio
  • Talks directly to each site's VERA for real answers
  • Generates executive portfolio reports on demand
  • Board-ready health and performance summaries
  • Proactive briefings to ownership before they ask
  • Energy performance benchmarking across sites
  • Can be branded and named for the client's company

Platform Features

Replace five tools
with one platform.

Overview replaces your NOC monitoring contract, your BAS service dashboards, your tenant ticketing system, your manual reporting process, and your reactive dispatch workflow — with one intelligent platform that actually understands buildings.

🏥

Live Health Scores

Every site gets a real-time health score from 0–100%. Not based on connectivity — based on what VERA actually found when she checked every point. Click any score to open a live conversation with VERA about what's happening and why.

🔍

AI Fault Detection

VERA scans every device and point every 15 minutes and reasons about the data like an experienced operator — catching multi-system faults, anomalies, and failure precursors that rule-based threshold systems miss entirely.

🧠

Site Memory

Every fault, fix, and equipment quirk gets written to a per-site memory file. Next time VERA sees the same pattern, she says "this happened in March — the belt was worn, check it first." Real institutional knowledge that never walks out the door.

📬

Proactive Alerts

VERA emails the right people in plain language before anyone picks up the phone. Specific, actionable, and sent from vera@overviewops.com — your clients start treating her like a real member of your team who's always on the job.

💬

Chat With Your Buildings

Every VERA and every portfolio agent has a full chat interface. Ask "why did the health score drop?" or "what's AHU-1 doing right now?" and get a real answer from an AI that knows that specific building's complete history.

📈

Portfolio Intelligence

Which of your 10 buildings needs attention this week? How did your portfolio perform this month? Which sites are candidates for a proactive client call? Overview answers every one of these questions in seconds with full context.

🌐

Fleet Knowledge Base

Every fault VERA finds gets evaluated for fleet-wide relevance. Equipment failure patterns, BACnet driver workarounds, and fault signatures that precede common failures — shared across your entire fleet so every new site benefits from day one.

🎫

Tenant Ticketing Coming

A full tenant work order and service request platform built into Overview. Tenants submit requests, VERA triages them against live building data, and your team gets dispatched with full context already attached. No more Angus. No more disconnected systems.

🔧

Work Order Management Coming

Create, assign, and close work orders with full building context attached to every job. VERA knows what's wrong before the tech arrives. Every work order is linked to the fault that created it and the history of that equipment.

⚡

Energy Intelligence Coming

VERA spots energy waste the same way she spots faults — by knowing what normal consumption looks like. Automated energy reports, anomaly detection, and cost savings recommendations delivered to ownership automatically every month.

🚗

Tech Dispatch Optimization Coming

Tell Overview who's available and it recommends which tech should go where based on site proximity, equipment familiarity, fault severity, and history. Dispatch that actually makes sense instead of whoever answers first.

📋

Executive Reporting Coming

Board-ready monthly reports generated automatically for every client — building performance, fault history, energy trends, and full service summary — delivered as a branded PDF your clients will actually read and value.

Platform Support

Works over any BAS platform.

Overview is vendor-agnostic by design. VERA connects through a standardized data adapter — she never cares what BAS platform she's running on top of, only what the points are saying. Add new platforms without changing anything about how VERA works.
