Platform Features

Replace five tools
with one platform.

Overview replaces your NOC monitoring contract, your Angus work order system, your manual reporting process, your energy consultant, and your reactive dispatch workflow — with one intelligent platform that actually understands your buildings.

🏥

Live Health Scores

Every site gets a real-time health score from 0–100%. Not based on connectivity — based on what VERA actually checked. Click any score to open a live voice or text chat with VERA about exactly what's happening and why.

🔍

AI Fault Detection

VERA uses smart relative rules — not brittle static thresholds. "Alert if zone temp exceeds setpoint by 3°C for 15 minutes." When the setpoint changes, the rule automatically updates. No reconfiguration ever needed.

🧠

Permanent Site Memory

Every fault, fix, and equipment quirk written to a permanent memory. VERA remembers the March belt failure when she sees the same pattern in October. Real institutional knowledge that never retires or walks out the door.

🔕

Zero Alert Fatigue

When RTU-3 fails, VERA sends one alert — not 47. She knows the four VAVs it feeds will alarm and suppresses them automatically. Every alert VERA sends is worth reading. That's what makes your team trust her.

👥

Smart Contact Rules

Robert gets one plain-English email at 9am. Mike gets an immediate push notification. Safety emergencies override everything. VERA remembers every preference, every time, without ever being told twice.

📈

Portfolio Intelligence

Which of your 10 buildings needs attention this week? How did your portfolio perform vs last month? Overview answers all of this in seconds — with the context to act on it, not just the numbers.

🌐

Fleet Knowledge Base

Every fault VERA finds gets evaluated for fleet relevance. Failure signatures that precede common faults are shared across your entire fleet automatically. Every new site benefits from everything every other site has learned.

🎫

Tenant Ticketing

A full tenant work order and service request platform built into Overview. Tenants submit requests, VERA triages against live building data, your team is dispatched with full context already attached. No more Angus.

⚡

Energy Intelligence

VERA spots energy waste the same way she spots faults — by knowing what normal looks like. Automated reports, anomaly detection, and savings recommendations delivered to ownership automatically every month.

🔧

Work Order Management

Create, assign, and close work orders with full building context attached to every job. VERA knows what's wrong before the tech arrives. Every work order linked to the fault that created it and the equipment history.

🚗

Tech Dispatch

Smart dispatch recommendations based on proximity, equipment familiarity, fault severity, and site history. Send the right tech to the right site instead of whoever picks up the phone first.

📋

Executive Reporting

Board-ready monthly reports generated automatically for every client — building performance, fault history, energy trends, full service summary. A branded document your clients will actually read and value.
